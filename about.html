About Greenwood Academy

Discover our rich history and commitment to educational excellence

Our Mission

To provide a comprehensive, challenging, and supportive educational environment that empowers students to become critical thinkers, effective communicators, and responsible global citizens.

Our Vision

To be a leading educational institution that inspires lifelong learning, fosters innovation, and prepares students to make meaningful contributions to society.

Our History

Founded in 1974, Greenwood Academy has been a cornerstone of educational excellence for over five decades. What began as a small private school with just 50 students has grown into a prestigious institution serving over 1,200 students from kindergarten through grade 12.

Our founders, Dr. Margaret Greenwood and Professor James Greenwood, envisioned a school that would combine academic rigor with character development. Their legacy continues today as we maintain the highest standards of education while adapting to the evolving needs of our students and society.

Throughout our history, we have consistently achieved outstanding academic results, with 98% of our graduates gaining admission to their first-choice universities. Our alumni have gone on to become leaders in various fields, including medicine, law, business, technology, and public service.
